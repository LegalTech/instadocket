Notifications

Instant Docket Updates

Instadocket.com is a third party e-mail notification system built exclusively for MassCourts.org. As a practicing attorney and litigator in the Commonwealth I grew frustrated with constantly having to check the court website for docket updates or worrying that I would miss an upcoming court date. Instadocket solves those problems by checking for you. Sign up for Instadocket.com and all updates to your docket will be automatically sent to you.

Event Reminders

Your Insurance Will Thank You

Instadocket provides your office with a reminder system for upcoming Open court events. Instadocket will check your MassCourts Events for any upcoming court dates. Simply choose how many days in advance you want to be reminded and we handle the rest.
